Frozen Pizza

Description

So you need a recipe for a frozen pizza? I mean sure, here you go, I guess?

Ingredients

  • Frozen Pizza
  • Oven (you have one of these, right?)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 225C or 400F
  2. Set the pizza directly on the rack and cook for 15 minutes
  3. Take pizza out of oven, and place on a cutting board or pizza stone
  4. Slice the pizza into 6 to 8 pieces, preferably with a pizza cutter
  5. Serve on a plate an enjoy
  6. Question your life decisions about needing to lookup how to cook a frozen pizza.
