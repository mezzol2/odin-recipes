Bacon Scrambled Eggs

Description

Elevate your breakfast with our delectable bacon and cheese scrambled eggs. Crispy bacon bit blended into fluffy eggs with a generous sprinkle of melted cheese adds a creamy richness. This harmonious blend of savory ingredients creates mouthwatering flavors, perfect for a gourmet morning indulgence.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Diced Bacon
  • Shredded Cheese

Steps

  1. Warm a pan to medium head and melt some butter.
  2. Add the raw bacon and cook until crispy.
  3. Add eggs and cheese. Stir them together until well mixed.
  4. Add salt and pepper for taste.
